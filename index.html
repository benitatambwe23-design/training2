Beef tacos

Beef tacos with shredded beef, tomatoes, red onions, green peppers, fresh herbs, and creamy sauce on a plate. onceuponachef. Available at: https://www.onceuponachef.com/recipes/beef-tacos.html

ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • taco shels of soft tortillas
  • 1 tablespon olive oil
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • salt and pepper
  • Shredded cheese
  • lettuce
  • tomato

Equipment

  • frying pan
  • Wooden spoonn or spatula
  • knife
  • Cutting board

Directions>

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and cook until soft.
  3. Add chopped onion and cook until soft.
  4. Add chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  5. Stir well and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Warm the taco shells.
  7. Fill shells with beef and add toppings.
